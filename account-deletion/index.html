This page explains how to delete your FabricWise account and associated data.

Option A — Delete in the app

  1. Open FabricWise and sign in.
  2. Tap the account icon in the top bar to open the Account screen.
  3. Scroll to the Danger zone.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Type the confirmation word shown on screen to confirm.
  6. Your account and data are permanently deleted, and you are signed out.

Option B — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email elitefutureunplugged@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject "Account deletion request". We will verify your request and delete your account. We aim to process verified requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and login, including your email/authentication record.
  • Your wardrobe and garment data.
  • Your homes and household profiles.
  • Your washing machines and washing programs.
  • Your wash-session history and saved outfits.
  • All images you uploaded, including garment, care-label, machine, and profile photos.
  • Your usage and entitlement records.

What is retained

For legal, financial, and fraud-prevention reasons, we keep anonymized billing and audit records of past transactions. After deletion, these records no longer identify you because the user reference is removed: billing_events.user_id is set to NULL.

Important: Google Play subscription

Deleting your FabricWise account does not cancel any active Google Play subscription. To stop future charges, you must cancel your subscription separately in Google Play: open the Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → FabricWise → Cancel. Cancelling a subscription and deleting your account are independent actions.
