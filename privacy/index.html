Privacy Policy — FabricWise

Effective date: 2026-06-19
Operator: Seckin Ozaltin ("we", "us", "our")
Contact: elitefutureunplugged@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains what data the FabricWise mobile app ("FabricWise", the "app") collects, how we use it, who processes it on our behalf, and your choices.

Who we are

FabricWise is operated by Seckin Ozaltin. For any privacy question or request, contact us at elitefutureunplugged@gmail.com.

Information we collect

Account information. When you create an account you provide an email address and password. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth. We store your email to identify your account and to communicate with you about it.

Wardrobe and garment data. Information you add about your clothing and laundry, including garment names, categories, fabric and color descriptions, care-label details such as wash temperature, spin, ironing, brand, size, and optional shopping details you choose to enter, such as price, currency, store, purchase link, order number, and dates. It also includes homes, household profiles you create, washing machines and their programs, and wash-session history.

Images. Photos you upload of garments, care labels, washing machines, and optional profile avatars. Images are stored in private Supabase Storage, organized so that each user's files are isolated to their own folder.

AI analysis. When you use AI features such as garment scan, care-label scan, machine scan, wash plans, or outfit suggestions, the relevant images and/or garment and weather text are sent to Anthropic Claude through our secure server-side functions so the model can extract care information or generate suggestions. Our AI provider processes this content to return results. The AI provider's API key is held only on our servers and never in the app.

Location and weather. If you use outfit suggestions, the app may request foreground approximate location permission to determine local weather, including reverse-geocoding your coordinates to a place name, so suggestions match current conditions. FabricWise may use your approximate foreground location only when needed for weather-based outfit suggestions. The app does not collect precise location and does not use background location. You can decline this permission; the feature then falls back to manually entered or unavailable weather.

Subscription and purchase status. If you subscribe to FabricWise Plus, purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat. We receive and store subscription status information such as your entitlement tier, store, product identifier, subscription state, and an associated subscription user identifier. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Device-local preferences. Settings such as relaxing-music on/off and selected track, theme, and language are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Microphone and audio. FabricWise does not record audio, collect microphone data, or request microphone permission. Optional in-app relaxing music playback does not use the microphone.

How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the app, including your wardrobe, laundry plans, outfit suggestions, and history.
  • To run AI features you request.
  • To provide local weather-based suggestions if you allow location access.
  • To manage your subscription and entitlements.
  • To secure the service, prevent abuse, and enforce usage limits.
  • To respond to your support and privacy requests.

Service providers

We use the following providers to operate FabricWise:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, file storage, and serverless Edge Functions hosting.
  • Anthropic — AI processing of images and text for AI features.
  • RevenueCat and Google Play Billing — subscription management and payment processing.
  • Expo — app build and delivery.
  • A weather data provider (Open-Meteo) — to return forecasts for your location.

How your data is stored and protected

Your data is isolated per user through row-level security, and images are kept in a private storage bucket scoped to your account folder. AI requests are made server-side so credentials are never exposed in the app. Data is transmitted using encrypted connections such as HTTPS/TLS where applicable. We retain your data while your account exists.

Account deletion and retention

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Account → Danger zone → Delete account in the app, or by emailing us. See our Account Deletion page. Deleting your account removes your account record, wardrobe data, uploaded images, and history.

For legal, financial, and fraud-prevention purposes, we retain anonymized billing and audit records: when an account is deleted, the user link on those billing records is removed (billing_events.user_id is set to NULL), so the records no longer identify you.

Deleting your account does not cancel any active Google Play subscription. You must cancel it separately in Google Play.

Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data. The fastest way to delete everything is the in-app deletion flow; you may also contact us at elitefutureunplugged@gmail.com. As we operate under the laws of Poland and the EU, you have rights under the GDPR, including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with the Polish supervisory authority, the President of the Personal Data Protection Office, UODO.

Children

FabricWise is not directed to children and is not intended for use by children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we will revise the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
